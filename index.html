Valentine's Day 2025

My dearest love,

Through all of life's twists and turns, my heart has never wavered in knowing one truth: you are my home. Since that magical Halloween in 2010, you've been the one who makes my world complete.

I love the little things about you that make my heart skip a beat - like those sweet little yips in your breath that catch me by surprise and make me fall in love all over again.

The time we spent apart only made me understand more deeply how precious what we share truly is. Like a compass needle always finding north, my soul always finds its way back to you.

This Valentine's Day, I'm not just asking you to be my Valentine - I'm asking you to continue this beautiful journey of rediscovery with me. Every day I find new reasons to fall in love with you.

You're not just my wife - you're my best friend, my partner, my Halloween anniversary sweetheart. Your strength inspires me, your kindness humbles me, and your love makes me want to be better every day.

So, my love, my forever Halloween treat, will you be my Valentine? Not just for one day, but for all the days to come? Let's write the next chapters of our story together.

With all my love and hope for our tomorrow,
**Joe**
P.S. Thank you for making every day since 10/31/10 better than the last.
